# CIVVEO - MAIN VIABLE PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

## Problem Statement

In today’s fast-paced digital world, local businesses struggle to gain visibility and to connect with their target audience. Local businesses face significant challenges in reaching the right customers and effectively marketing their products. 

While platforms like Amazon, Flipkart and Blinkit dominate online shopping, they often come at a cost- time, delivery fees and most importantly, the slow fading of local shopkeepers. Blinkit may bring groceries in minutes, but it also quietly replaces the trusted local kirana shop that has served communities for generations. 

Yet, despite this digital wave, most of the people, still in India, shops locally from toothpaste to tools, paints to phone chargers. Why? Because Indian consumers trust their local shopkeepers, the personal connections, immediate availability and reliability are unmatched. 

However, a modern-day gap remains. Customers today search for products online, walk into stores with screenshots and often leave disappointed because they don’t know which nearby shop actually has what they need. Shopkeepers, on the other hand, struggle to be discovered in this digital-life era. 

In short, customers can’t find what’s near them and shopkeepers can’t reach the customers already around them. 

This invisible gap between the local buyer and the local seller is costing both- in time, trust and opportunity. 

## Solution

Civveo bridges the gap between local shopkeepers and nearby customers through a location-based digital marketplace built on social connectivity. 

Shopkeepers can easily create digital storefronts, lists their products and update availability in real time with no technical skills required. Customers, in turn, can search for any product within a few kilometers ratio, view where it’s available, compare prices, read local reviews and directly chat with shopkeepers. 

Thus, local shopkeepers will be having a platform where they can promote their products to reach to the local customers. And in essence, customers will find a desired product only in their nearby area without any delivery charges or a stress of how the products will be, when they are delivered. 

So, By combining e-commerce efficiency with community trust, Civveo transforms local markets into smart, connected ecosystems. 

> “Civveo doesn’t replace local stores, it revives them.”

Giving locals a modern digital presence while ensuring customers get what they need instantly and locally. 

## Target Audience / User Persona

There are two main users of CIVVEO: 

### 1. Local Shopkeepers 
**Local Business Owners want more visibility & customers.**
Small and local shop owners often rely on: Word of mouth and walk-in customers. 

They struggle because: Their shop is not visible online, people nearby don’t know what products they offer, they can’t compete with online marketplaces in visibility. 

On Civveo, they can sign in and promote their products which will help them gain nearby customers. They can lists their products, stocks, post the product images or videos and advertise them to attract local/nearby customers. Also they can use their local language in Civveo. Hence, there is no need of more technical knowledge. Which makes them use it efficiently. 

### 2. Customers 
**Customers want to shop offline but struggle to find products easily.**
Many customers still prefer visiting physical stores because they can touch, feel, and immediately buy the product. 

However, the biggest challenge they face is discoverability — they don’t know which nearby shop has the product they want, what varieties are available, or whether it's in stock. 

So, on Civveo, they can find products available nearby in local shops. Also, they can check whether the product is in stock, they can communicate with local shopkeeper, also watch out the local reviews and civveo also guide them to the exact location through map. 

## Core MVP Features

Here are core main viable product features: 

1. **Simple Login** 
   - Users (both shopkeepers and customers) can quickly log in using basic credentials like phone number, email or OTP. It ensures secure access and lets users save their data. 

2. **Role selection: Shopkeeper / Customer** 
   - Users can select their roles whether they are shopkeepers or customer. Shopkeeper gets access to product posting and listing and shop profile. While customers get access to browse, search and view products. 

3. **Shopkeeper product listing + catalog page** 
   - Helps Shopkeepers display everything they sell in a clean, organized format. They can add all product details and all the listed products will be shown at one place. 

4. **Feed/home with product cards + top review** 
   - Helps customers discover trending or popular products easily. Product card shows product price, image, shop, etc. And top reviews section refers to best rated products. 

5. **Search page for people + shopkeepers** 
   - Helps easy navigation to find a specific shop or product quickly. Customers can search for products, shopkeepers and shops in their areas. 

6. **Search bar in feed for products with filters** 
   - A search bar on the main feed page helps user find products making searches more accurate and personalized. 

7. **Product Page (coming from feed/search/shop)** 
   - A dedicated page for each product that opens when clicked. It gives all the information a customer needs before buying or visiting the shop. 

8. **Reviews on the product** 
   - Helps build trust and lets customer make informed decision. Customers can see the reviews written by the other users and add their own reviews. 

9. **Map icon (show shop location)** 
   - Allows customers to know exactly where the shop is and navigate easily. Each shop or product page include a map icon. Clicking it opens the shop’s location on map. 

10. **QR code for every profile** 
    - Helps in quick sharing, easy access and offline-to-online connection (scanning QR in real markets). Both customers and shopkeepers get a unique QR code. 

## User Flow and Journey

### User Flow for Shopkeepers:
1. Opens App 
2. Sign up/ Log in (if account already exists) 
3. Select Role - Shopkeeper (in case of sign up) 
4. Post their products/ Lists their products 
5. Manages Catalog 
6. Share profile QR 
7. View Customer reviews 
8. Update Product details 
9. Chat with customer (if needed) 
10. Update stock (to available or not available) 
11. Exit 

### User Flow for Customers:
1. Opens App 
2. Sign up/ Log in (if account already exists) 
3. Select Role - Customer (in case of sign up) 
4. Home feed opens 
5. Customer searches or browse products 
6. Opens a product page 
7. Read reviews 
8. Views shop location on map 
9. Optionally sees shopkeepers profile/ contact with shopkeeper 
10. Exit
