## 💡 Product Overview

**Neargrab** bridges the invisible gap between local shopkeepers and nearby customers through a location-based digital marketplace built on social connectivity. 

While fast-delivery and mega e-commerce platforms continue to dominate online shopping, they come at the cost of the slow fading of trusted local community shopkeepers. Neargrab aims to give local sellers a modern digital presence, ensuring customers get what they need instantly and locally. 

*"Neargrab doesn’t replace local stores, it revives them."*

### Key Challenges Solved
* **For Local Businesses:** Struggling to reach digital-first buyers nearby; unable to compete with the visibility of giant online marketplaces.
* **For Customers:** Leaving stores disappointed because they do not know prior to visiting whether a nearby shop holds a particular product in stock.

### The Solution Ecosystem
A low-friction platform providing local shopkeepers with simple, digital storefronts that can be updated in real-time with zero technical skills (and native language support). Customers can comfortably search a designated kilometer radius, assess product availability, compare local prices, view reviews, and navigate to local shops immediately.

## 🚀 Core MVP Features

1. **Effortless Onboarding**: Secure and simple login using Mobile/Email/OTP.
2. **Dual-Role Environments**: Dedicated, specialized access for *Shopkeepers* vs *Customers*.
3. **Shopkeeper Digital Catalog**: A clean, organized format for local businesses to list everything they sell.
4. **Interactive Local Feed**: A discovery home page featuring product cards and top-rated item reviews.
5. **Robust Search Engine**: Discover specific shops, people, or products with advanced filters.
6. **Dedicated Product Pages**: Contains all information a customer needs before visiting the physical store.
7. **Map Integration**: Real-world navigational tools directing foot traffic directly to the physical storefront.
8. **QR Code Sharing**: Unique QR codes for every profile to create an instant offline-to-online bridge in local markets.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **Local Shopkeepers**: Small business owners wanting local digital visibility, easy tools for community advertising, and tools to boost foot traffic.
- **Local Customers**: Community members wanting to physically pick up products same-day nearby, trusting immediate availability over waiting for online deliveries.
